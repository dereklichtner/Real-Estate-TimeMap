U.S. Real Estate TimeMap

The price of a home has changed in the last few years.

This interactive map shows the ups and downs of the real estate market since 1996. Here's how to start exploring the data:

  • Press to start the TimeMap animation
  • Press to step forward by one year
  • Press to step backward by one year
  • Drag the SLIDER on the timeline to manually change the year
  • Select any metropolitan area (
    ) on the map for more details
  • The size and color of each point reflect the typical home price that year:
    $35,000  
      $1,750,000
  • Press to show a graph of price vs. time for the selected city

Map design and development by Derek T. Lichtner. TimeMap animation and related controls are custom extensions to the OpenLayers project. Real estate data from Zillow.
Map last updated August 2022.
